Photography Competition Rules

Important: Registration closes on May 20th. Any registrations after this date will be considered invalid.

Registration Requirements

  • Complete the registration form below
  • Submit a confirmation payment screenshot after payment
  • Join the official WhatsApp group (link will be provided after registration)
  • Registration deadline: May 20th

Photography Guidelines

  • All photographs must be taken by the participant themselves
  • No stock photos or images taken by others are allowed
  • Images must be original and authentic

Competition Rules

  • Strictly no tolerance for bot likes or fake impressions
  • Any participant found using fake likes or impressions will be immediately disqualified
  • All engagement must be organic and genuine
  • Participants must maintain fair play throughout the competition

Disqualification Rules

  • Using fake likes or impressions
  • Submitting photos not taken by the participant
  • Not joining the WhatsApp group
  • Registering after May 20th
  • Not submitting payment confirmation screenshot
